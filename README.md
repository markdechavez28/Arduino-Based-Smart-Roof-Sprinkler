The objectives of the project are the following:
1. To design and develop an Arduino-based automated roof sprinkler system that can be programmed to activate based on environmental conditions such as temperature, smoke, or time scheduling.
2. To reduce roof surface temperature by automatically spraying water during extreme heat, helping improve indoor comfort and minimize heat absorption.
3. To enhance fire prevention and safety by activating the sprinkler system when abnormal heat or smoke levels are detected on the roof area.
4. To optimize water usage by using sensors and controlled activation, ensuring that the sprinkler operates only when necessary and avoids water wastage.
5. To demonstrate the integration of sensors, relays, and actuators in an IoT-ready Arduino system for smart home or building applications.

The following are the proposed components of the project:
1. Arduino Uno
2. DHT22
3. Water Pump 
4. Power Supply
5. Jumper Wires
6. Breadboard
7. Sprinkler / Hose
